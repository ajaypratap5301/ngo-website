About Us

Helping Hands is a non-profit organisation working to improve education, healthcare, and sustainable livelihoods in underserved communities. We partner with local leaders to design programs that are culturally appropriate and measurable.

  • Community-run education programs
  • Primary health camps & awareness
  • Skill training & micro-enterprise support

Our Programs

Education

After-school centers, scholarships and teacher training to keep children in school.

Health & Nutrition

Community health camps, nutrition counselling and maternal care awareness.

Livelihoods

Skill-building workshops and microloan support for small businesses.
